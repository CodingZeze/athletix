# Vercel Deployment Guide

## Step 1: Create Vercel Account
1. Go to **https://vercel.com**
2. Click **"Sign Up"**
3. Select **"Continue with GitHub"**
4. Authorize Vercel to access your GitHub account

## Step 2: Import Project
1. After signing in, you'll see the dashboard
2. Click **"Add New..."** button
3. Click **"Project"**
4. Find **"athletix"** in your repositories
5. Click **"Import"**

## Step 3: Configure & Deploy
1. Vercel will auto-detect Next.js
2. Click **"Deploy"**
3. Wait 1-2 minutes for deployment
4. You'll get a URL like: **https://athletix-xyz.vercel.app**

## Step 4: Your Live Website

Paste your Vercel URL into any browser - your website is now live! 🎉

## That's It!

Your AthletiX website is now live on the internet.
